About Wildkidzz

My name is Ferdinand van der Kuip, I am the designer and producer of Wildkidzz furniture.

How we make it?

We have been producing kinderbedden, kinderkasten and kindermeubels since 1996 in Arnhem.

In 20 years we have accumulated a lot of experience in designing and producing fun, safe and sturdy kinder furniture.

Unique design

All my projects are unique design — there is no furniture like that anywhere else in the world. More importantly, they are fun for kids. Children love our pieces, they are more happy in their rooms.

I also often make beds and chests that are specially customized to the specific room or vision by parents or kids. If you have something in mind that you would like to make, please give me a ring or send your sketch, and we will see whether we can produce it.

Our partners

I work with a talented artists, Manon Elmendrop, who produce colorful, customized pieces for our furniture. You can visit her at her website or Instagram, and if you like what you see, you can send us a request and we will work with Manon to put it up.

We provide furniture delivery and assembly free of charge, to ensure that things are as fast as possible in your kinder kamers, and everything is taken care of.

We also provide lifetime guarantee for our pieces. It is not difficult to do – everything is very well put together, hardly anything ever breaks, and if it does , we will come and fix it.

If you like to discuss some kids project, please give me a call.

Do you have any questions?

We will be happy to discuss and answer any of your questions.
