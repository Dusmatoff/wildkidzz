How we make it?

Leather goods WildKidzz can their national history. I want everything to be in order, our family grew in expectation of people for people.

A lot of parents look online for inspiration to decorate their children’s rooms. My favourite platform at the moment is Instagram. It’s not only about beautiful images, but also personal style and touch which I like the most. You can just travel all around the world to see children’s rooms! How great is that! I discovered a mom of two Mika on Instagram too.

Our whole bed collection

We have three beds for different children ages:

  • XS — Extra Small;
  • S — Small;
  • M — Medium;
  • L — Large.

You can always use them separately, but the unique feature of the collection is, that you can combine them when the kids need to share a room. If you look not only for a practical solution but quality and aesthetics are important too – welcome in the club! Rafa-kids beds solve the problem of kids sharing a room – but also look modern and beautiful.

What do you think about

The very beginning of Rafa-kids was finding a solution for accommodating two kids in one room. We studied the issue, sketched and brainstormed for a long time.

The Sianzeng Dino Wallpaper

I adore this room with the Dinosaur theme. The neutral background of the wall, floor and furniture make these fun dino prints stand out, and give you the chance to give the room a character. Rafa-kids XL shelf limited edition matches so well with the neutral color scheme of the room.

If your child collects dinos (or any other things), it would be great to give them a right spot in the room. A shelf dedicated to exposing the favorite elements of a collection will do the job so well! If there is more, store it in a beautiful basket or drawers, so the child can take it out while playing and hide it afterward.

  1. We want our furniture range to complement each other;
  2. Beds, shelves, benches, and tables work on its own of course, but also in sets. Rafa-kids storage needed to fit right in the middle of the collection;
  3. The challenge was on! We sketched, build models, tested prototypes.
A wooden floor gives a great base for white furniture, which make it more fresh and modern. It’s not only about beautiful images, but also personal style and touch which I like the most.
